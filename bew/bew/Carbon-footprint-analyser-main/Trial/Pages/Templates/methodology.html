Methodology

To determine the carbon footprint of coal mining, begin by defining assessment boundaries, including operational scope, geographical limits, and time frame. The calculation encompasses emissions from mining, energy use, transportation, and waste management.

Emission Calculation Methodology

1. Coal Production Emissions

Estimate CO₂ emissions by multiplying annual coal output with factors for energy content, exclusions, and CO₂ per energy unit.

2. Mine-Type Emissions

  • Open-Cast Mines: Account for diesel used in overburden removal and carbon loss from land disturbance.
  • Underground Mines: Calculate methane (CH₄) emissions, converted to CO₂ equivalents using global warming potential (GWP).

3. Equipment & Fuel Emissions

Include CO₂ from diesel machinery, explosives, and electricity, factoring in regional emission rates.

4. Transportation Emissions

Use transport mode-specific factors to calculate emissions from coal and waste transport distances.

5. Waste Management

Address emissions from refuse transport and disposal.

Finally, sum emissions across all stages, adjusting for region-specific factors and uncertainties for accurate, repeatable results. This method ensures detailed tracking of emissions and encourages transparency in carbon footprint assessments.

Formulae

Land disturbance:
CO₂ = Area Disturbed (ha) × Carbon Stock Loss (tonnes CO₂/ha)
Methane emissions:
CO₂ = CH₄ released (m³) × Methane Emission Factor × (16.04 / 22.914) × 1000
Where 16.04 is the molecular weight of methane and 22.914 is the molar volume at standard conditions

Equipment & Fuel Emission:

A) Diesel Powered Machinery:
CO₂ = Diesel Used (liters) × Avg Emission Factor for all machines (kg CO₂/liter)
B) Explosives:
CO₂ = Explosives Used (kg) × Emission Factor (kg CO₂/kg)
C) Electricity Used:
CO₂ = Electricity (MWh) × Grid Emission Factor (kg CO₂/MWh)
Transport:
CO₂ = Distance (km) × Coal Weight (tonnes) × Emission Factor (kg CO₂/tonne km)
Carbon Sequestration Potential:
CO₂ = Area reforested (ha) × Carbon Sequestration Rate (tonnes CO₂/ha/year)
